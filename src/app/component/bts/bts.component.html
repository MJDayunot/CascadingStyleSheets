ABOUT BTS

BTS, also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan or Beyond the Scene, is a South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. Since their debut in 2013, they have become one of the most popular music groups in the world, known for their impactful lyrics and performances.

Members

  • RM (Leader)
  • Jin
  • Suga
  • J-Hope
  • Jimin
  • V
  • Jungkook

Achievements

BTS has received numerous awards, including Billboard Music Awards, American Music Awards, and has broken multiple records on streaming platforms.
